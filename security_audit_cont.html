Security & Audit Trail - Audit & Incident Response

Comprehensive audit logging and incident response capabilities ensuring complete traceability, regulatory compliance, and rapid threat mitigation

Audit Trail Features
  • Comprehensive logging of all user actions, system events, and data modifications
  • Immutable audit records with cryptographic hashing preventing tampering
  • Timestamp accuracy using NTP synchronization for forensic analysis
  • User attribution tracking who, what, when, where, and why for every action
  • Searchable audit logs with advanced filtering and export capabilities
  • Compliance reporting for SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and industry standards
  • Retention management with configurable retention periods and archival

Incident Response

  • Automated detection of suspicious activities and anomalous behavior patterns
  • Real-time alerting via email, SMS, and integration with SIEM platforms
  • Incident classification with severity levels and escalation procedures
  • Containment procedures including account suspension and access revocation
  • Forensic investigation tools with complete audit trail reconstruction
  • Recovery procedures with documented playbooks and rollback capabilities
  • Post-incident review with root cause analysis and preventive measures
Compliance Standards
Architecture designed to meet SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and industry-specific regulatory requirements with regular third-party audits and certifications.
