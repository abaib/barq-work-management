User Roles & Permissions - Part 2

Continuation of specialized roles covering HSE Officer, Warehouse Manager, Finance Manager, and Viewer/Analyst with their key responsibilities and primary module access.

HSE Officer

Key Responsibilities
  • Safety compliance and incident investigation
  • Audit management and corrective actions
  • Regulatory reporting and trend analysis
Primary Modules
  • QC & HSE Compliance, Reporting & Analytics

Warehouse Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Inventory control and stock management
  • Material requisition approval
  • Supplier coordination and receiving
Primary Modules
  • Material Management, Work Orders, Reporting

Finance Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Financial oversight and budget management
  • Invoice processing and payment approval
  • Cost analysis and financial reporting
Primary Modules
  • Financial Management, Contract Management, Reporting

Viewer/Analyst

Key Responsibilities
  • Read-only access for monitoring and analysis
  • Dashboard viewing and report generation
  • Data analysis and trend identification
Primary Modules
  • Reporting & Analytics (limited access to other modules)
